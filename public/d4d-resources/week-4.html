Week 4: Color

Video

This weeks video is all about color, how to choose color for your designs and implementing color for the screen.

Workshop

This week you should be concentrating on the color on the site. How color fits in with you hierarchy, your type and the overall style of the design.

Office Hours

This weeks office hours we will have a group critique of where you all are after refining the color in your project. While giving critique, I want you all to continue to use visual principles and the color language that was in the video to back up your critique and design decisions. Feel free to use the cheatsheet while you do your critique.

This is a great time to ask me about specific color choices or color tools that you might look to use or want to use. Also feel free to ask me about Sass color tips and using RGBa.
