Week 3: Typography

Video

This weeks video is all about typography, setting type and choosing typefaces for your design.

Workshop

This week you should be concentrating on the type on the site. Making sure that it flows with the rest of the hierarchy of the site. Make sure you are putting emphasis on the right chances of content. Make sure the type style is saying what you want it to say. You should still be keeping everything grayscale and not introducing color just yet.

Office Hours

This weeks office hours we will have a group critique of where you all are after refining the typography in your project. While giving critique, I want you all to continue to use visual principles and the new type terms to back up your critique and design decisions. Feel free to use the cheatsheet while you do your critique.

This is a great time to ask me about specific typefaces, implementing @font-face or any other type related questions.
