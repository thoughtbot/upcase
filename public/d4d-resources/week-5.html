Video

This weeks video is all about recent trends in design, some small tricks to think about when designing and some practices that you should follow as designers.

Workshop

This is your chance to refine your design. Keep iterating on small pieces of the design till the design as a whole fits great together.

Office Hours

This weeks office hours we will have a group critique of the final state of your project. While giving critique, I want you all to continue to use all of the design language that you've learned up to this point to back up your critique and design decisions. Feel free to use any of the cheatsheets while you do your critique.

Give me any final questions that you might have on any of the previous subjects. Let me know if there was something that you wanted me to cover that I didn't or something that you want me to talk more about.
